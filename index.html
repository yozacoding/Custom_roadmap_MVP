🧠 Who it's for

This service is designed for creatives, neurodivergent people, and anyone who is either:

  • Juggling multiple priorities and doesn’t know where to start
  • Feeling stuck, scattered, or overwhelmed and needs clarity
  • Wanting more structure but struggles with rigid routines
  • Open to gentle accountability and tailored planning

Whether you thrive with clear checklists and time blocks, or prefer a gentler rhythm with emotional safety at the core, we’ll build your plan around you

✅ What you'll get

  • A custom daily schedule designed around your natural energy patterns
  • A weekly focus roadmap aligned with your goals and intentions
  • Supportive tips for managing common blockers like procrastination and low energy
  • A beautifully designed version of your personalised plan, delivered in your preferred format (PDF, Google Doc, Obsidian note, Notion Template)

⚙️ How it works

  1. Fill out a short questionnaire
    Share a bit about your current routine, focus blocks, goals, and struggles
  2. We’ll craft your roadmap
    We use AI tools to help generate your personalised routine. Every plan is based on the answers you provide, and we review submissions to ensure quality and relevance. Within 48 hours, you'll receive an initial personalised plan, tailored to your lifestyle
  3. Track and adjust
    After the first week, we’ll jump on a 1-to-1 Zoom meeting to review your progress and tweak your plan as needed
